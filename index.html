Plant/Fruit diseases disrupt the normal plant growth and is one of the leading causes of lower production, which leads to economic losses. Early detection of disease aids in the development of treatments that can slow the spread of disease in plants. One of the reasons why plant leaf disease detection is so crucial in agriculture is because of this. Leaf inspection is regarded as one of the most effective methods for diagnosing plant diseases.
